# NARA Digital Ocean Platform
## Professional Website Valuation & Technical Analysis
**Prepared for: Fisheries Ministry & NARA Chairman**
**Date: January 2025**

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The NARA Digital Ocean Platform is an **enterprise-level government digital infrastructure** encompassing 80+ integrated web applications, 25+ administrative systems, and real-time maritime data services. Built with modern web technologies and following ICTA Guidelines v4.0 for Sri Lankan government websites.

### Key Highlights
- **80+ Public-Facing Pages** across 15 major portals
- **25+ Administrative Dashboards** for complete system management
- **Trilingual Support** (Sinhala, Tamil, English) - ICTA compliant
- **Real-Time Maritime Data Integration** from NASA, Stormglass, OpenWeather
- **AI-Powered Features** (ChatGPT, Google Gemini, AWS Polly)
- **Complete Digital Library System** with physical library management
- **E-Commerce Marketplace** with payment gateway integration
- **Advanced Analytics & Predictive Systems** for policy simulation

### Estimated Development Cost
**LKR 45,000,000 - 55,000,000** (USD 150,000 - 180,000)
**Development Timeline: 18-24 months** (by 6-8 person team)
